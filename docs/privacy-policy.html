Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

Overview

qBitController is a remote control client for qBittorrent. We take your privacy seriously.

Data Collection

qBitController does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or any third-party tracking services.

Data Stored on Device

The app stores your qBittorrent server address and credentials locally on your device using iOS standard storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Network Access

All network communication occurs exclusively between your device and your own qBittorrent server. No data is sent to any external server controlled by the developer.
